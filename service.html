Athlete Development

Our athlete development scheme is a unique concept. It is designed to allow athletes an opportunity to gain a high level of coaching helping them to increase their own performance qualities and develop them as an athlete. Whether you are competing locally or have aspirations to play at professional and/or international level, our athlete development sessions can help you improve. We want you to be the best you can be! At Sports Performance Services Ltd, we work closely with each athlete to improve their desired training outcome. We expect the athlete to show commitment to their cause and better themselves as an athlete. Our athlete development package includes the following;

  • 8 week training cycle, 2 S&C sessions per week. Appropriate progressions and adjustments made along the way
  • - Pre and post body composition monitoring- Skinfold & body fat analysis/circumference measurements/weight/height (optional)
  • Pre and post FMS (functional movement screening)
  • - Pre and post-performance testing. (strength/speed & agility/power).
  • Individualised training plan - Periodised training plans to include (and if required); Sufficient warm up, strength, speed and agility, reactive drills, technical honing, warm down/stretch
  • - Innovative cognitive development sessions (booked separately)
  • - Video analysis/feedback. (If required)
  • - Discounted sports massage service. (booked separately)
  • - FREE Training t-shirt
